How we work

From brief to
finished document,
without the wait

I

Project brief

You complete a short intake form covering your organisation, objectives, any existing documentation, and your deadline. This becomes the foundation of the work.

II

First draft

We produce a complete first draft, typically within 24–48 hours. You receive a structured document written to the appropriate professional and regulatory standard.

III

Review & refine

You provide feedback and we incorporate revisions. All work includes two rounds of amendments to ensure the final document fully meets your requirements.

IV

Final delivery

Your completed document is delivered as a polished Word or PDF file, ready for submission, publication, or client presentation. Invoice issued on delivery.
